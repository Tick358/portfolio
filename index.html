    Who am I?

    I starting music in 2020 during Covid-19 pandemic. I first learned to play the piano with music from Zelda and Undertale..Soon, I wanted to do more and compose originals musics. So I download the first music software I found, Musescore3. Unfortunately, it was a terrible program for composing, as it's intended to make music scores. . Furthermore my first musics was terrible. after which I switched to the LMMS software thanks to a friend and finally to FL Studio. Since then, I've been making musics several hours a day every day as well as doing a university degree in audiovisual music composition.

    What I do

    First, I worked on some student projects. Then with some friend we worked on a Deltarune Fangame project which began in 2020. I composed over 40 musics for this fangame. We were two composers so I learned to compose with others people. Now this project is over but I still have some projects like a musical album in duet, a little independent video game and obviously my youtube channel.

    What's my type of composing and my main inspirations?

    I can compose music in any style and any genre, but I have much more affinity for video games musics. Of course, I can also create music for short films or any sort of project that needs music.
    My mains inspirations are the musics of Undertale/Deltarune, The Legend of Zelda series, A Hat in Times, Outer Wdils, Celeste and When They Cry series.
    I make it a point to create Leitmotivs in my musics (you can hear some of them in my Portfolio) and if you ask me to put some on, I'll be able to.
